The editor in chief of UE Dawn, the official student publication of the University of the East, was allegedly forced by barangay officials in Nueva Ecija province to publicly apologize for criticizing the Duterte administration on social media, UE RedWire said.

According to RedWire, a public apology was demanded after the editor in chief’s high school teachers were “offended” by his statements against President Duterte. | Via Meg Adonis, Philippine Daily Inquirer
